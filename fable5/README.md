# WinUbuntu — the Fable 5 Edition

### *From Windows User to Confident AI Explorer*

> *"Too many technical books begin with commands. We begin with people."*

This is a second complete telling of the WinUbuntu journey — same destination, same three companions, written independently from the same source material as [the original edition](README.md). It exists so the two can be compared side by side.

A few things are different in this telling:

- **The fourth companion gets its full story.** The silent Terminal changes meaning in every mission — watch for the closing beat at the end of each one.
- **You keep a journal.** From Mission 4 onward, one honest line per mission. Mission 8 explains why — and pays it off.
- **The fears shrink on the page.** Each mission's "Elephant in the Room" is deliberately smaller than the last. That shrinking is the plot.
- **You break something on purpose.** Mission 5 includes your first error — planned, supervised, and repaired by your own hands — so error messages never frighten you again.
- **You cut the wire.** Mission 3 proves your AI is truly local the only way that's unforgettable: turn off the Wi-Fi and watch it keep thinking.

**By the end, with your own hands, you'll have:**

- 🐧 installed Ubuntu inside Windows,
- 🤖 run a real AI on your own machine — private, offline, free,
- 🐍 written your first program — and broken and repaired it,
- 🌍 joined the world where all software is shared, with your own work protected,
- 🔧 built a small app of your very own that talks to your own AI.

Our destination is not Ubuntu. **It's confidence.**
